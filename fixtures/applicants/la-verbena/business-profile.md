# La Verbena — Business Profile

Prepared by María Elena Vargas, Owner
Last updated: 2025-04-10

## Concept

La Verbena is a counter-service taquería on Mission Street serving
traditional Mexico City–style tacos, burritos, tortas, and house-made
aguas frescas. We focus on a tight menu, fresh masa, and quick service
for the lunch and dinner rush. We do **not** serve alcohol. We do **not**
operate a delivery fleet — third-party delivery is available only
through DoorDash and Uber Eats, who pick up orders at the counter.

## History

I (María Elena Vargas) registered Taquería La Verbena LLC with the
California Secretary of State on **March 30, 2022** (file # 202351410842)
and obtained the SF business registration on **April 12, 2022**. The
business effectively began operating on **April 18, 2022**.

The first location (2022–2024) was a small shared commissary kitchen at
2845 24th Street, where we built up the brand through farmers' markets,
catering, and a window-pickup operation. We moved into our current
storefront at **3128 Mission Street** on **May 1, 2024**, when the lease
on the previous space expired and the Mission Street space (a former
sandwich shop) became available.

## Premises — 3128 Mission Street

- Ground-floor storefront in a mixed-use building (apartments above).
- Approximately **900 sq ft** total leased area.
  - Front-of-house / dining: ~450 sq ft, **18 seats** (counter service).
  - Kitchen / prep / dish: ~380 sq ft.
  - Storage / dry goods / staff: ~70 sq ft.
- Hours: **11:00 to 22:00 daily.** Closed Thanksgiving and Christmas Day.
- No on-site parking; no drive-thru; no outdoor seating.

### Construction details

- Building constructed **1924**; mixed-use (residential above
  commercial).
- **Type V wood-frame** construction with stucco exterior on the
  street-facing facade and lath-and-plaster interior walls.
- **Composition shingle roof** (last re-roofed by landlord in 2019 per
  the lease disclosure).
- **Electrical:** main panel and tenant feed updated in 2018 (200A
  service). All kitchen circuits on dedicated breakers; Type-1 hood with
  ANSUL R-102 wet-chemical fire-suppression system (semi-annual
  inspection by Bay Area Fire Protection).
- **Plumbing:** building plumbing is largely original; landlord did a
  partial repipe of the ground-floor commercial run (galvanized →
  copper) in 2015 prior to the previous tenant moving in.
- **HVAC:** rooftop package unit serving the storefront only (~5 ton).
- **Sprinklers:** building is sprinklered (NFPA 13).
- **Fire/burglar alarm:** monitored alarm via Bay Cities Security
  (account # BCS-44210).

## Operations description (for ACORD)

Limited-service Mexican restaurant. The business operates a single
counter-service location selling prepared tacos, burritos, tortas, and
non-alcoholic beverages (aguas frescas, horchata, jamaica, bottled
soda) for on-premises dining and customer takeout. No table service. No
alcohol sold or served. No delivery employees — third-party delivery
platforms (DoorDash, Uber Eats) handle pickup at the counter. Occasional
small catering orders (drop-off only, within San Francisco) account for
about 3% of revenue.

## Menu (summary)

- **Tacos** (carnitas, al pastor, suadero, pollo, rajas con queso,
  hongos, frijoles).
- **Burritos** (same proteins; rice + beans + salsa + crema).
- **Tortas** (same proteins on a telera roll).
- **Sides:** chips, guacamole, rice, beans, elote.
- **Drinks:** aguas frescas (horchata, jamaica, tamarindo), Mexican
  Coke, Jarritos, sparkling water. **No beer, no wine, no spirits.**

## Risk-relevant notes

  commissary, $7,420 paid). After that incident we installed an
  upgraded Type-1 hood with ANSUL suppression at the new location and
  enrolled in a quarterly hood-cleaning program with Bay Area Fire
  Protection (vendor # BAFP-22-310).

  supply runs only.
- We do not host live music, special events, or after-hours rentals.
